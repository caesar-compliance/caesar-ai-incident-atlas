# Research Context — Caesar AI Incident Atlas

## Role

Incident-to-control knowledge base.

## What it should become

A structured dataset and searchable site mapping AI incidents to failure modes, controls, and evidence.

## Product type

- dataset
- taxonomy
- static site
- search/filter UI
- future training/risk module

## Main users

- consultants
- risk teams
- compliance teams
- trainers
- AI governance teams

## Main use cases

- explain why controls are needed
- map failures to controls
- map controls to evidence
- support client workshops
- support risk assessments

## MVP

- incident schema
- failure mode taxonomy
- controls taxonomy
- evidence mapping
- 10–20 sample incident records
- simple static site later

## Future paid use

- sector-specific risk library
- client training packs
- workshops
- control recommendations
- incident simulation material

## Promotion

Learn from AI failures. Map incidents to practical controls.
