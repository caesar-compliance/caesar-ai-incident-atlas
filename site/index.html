About

Caesar AI Incident Atlas is a curated AI incident knowledge base. Each record maps a real-world AI failure to failure modes, affected governance controls, required evidence, and governance lessons.

The governance mapping layer follows the pattern:

incident → failure mode → controls → evidence → lessons
